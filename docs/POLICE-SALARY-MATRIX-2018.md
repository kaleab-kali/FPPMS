## Salary Step Progression Rules

### Business Rules

1. **Step Increment Period**: Every **2 years** of service
2. **Total Steps**: 9 incremental steps per rank (plus base and ceiling)
3. **Maximum Progression**: Base Salary + 9 Steps = 11 salary levels per rank
4. **Ceiling Behavior**: Once an employee reaches the ceiling (Step 9), salary remains constant until rank change
5. **Rank Change**: Salary resets to base salary of new rank upon promotion (through appraisal)

### Step Calculation

```
Step 0 = Base Salary (Year 0-2)
Step 1 = After 2 years of service
Step 2 = After 4 years of service
Step 3 = After 6 years of service
Step 4 = After 8 years of service
Step 5 = After 10 years of service
Step 6 = After 12 years of service
Step 7 = After 14 years of service
Step 8 = After 16 years of service
Step 9 = After 18 years of service (Ceiling reached)
Step 9+ = Salary frozen at ceiling until promotion
```

### Time to Reach Ceiling
- **Minimum time at one rank to reach ceiling**: 18 years
- **After ceiling**: Employee must be promoted to get salary increase
